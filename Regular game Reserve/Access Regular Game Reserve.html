How to access

How to get to Regular Game Reserve Travelling to Regular Game Reserve Regular Game Reserve is accessible in a variety of ways. The reserve is easily accesible by car or plane.

Getting to our Game Reserve by air

Numerous flight options are available for those who want to visit Regular Game Reserve. South African Airways, Emirates Airways and Cemair offer flights Drakensburg Airport (DA) and King Shaka International Airport (KSIA). From all airports in the vicinity of the reserve it is possible to continue the journey to Regular Reserve lodges by means of a road transfer. We recommend flying directly from Johannesburg or Cape Town to Drakensberg as this is the nearest airport and is serviced multiple times per day. The road transfer from King Shaka to Drakensburg takes around one hour.

Regular Game Lodge hops

The most exciting way of arrival at Regular Game Lodge is by making use of a so called "lodge hop". This is an air transfer made by means of a small aircraft. These short charter flights may be arranged from Drakensburg Airport and King Shake International Airport. All lodges in Regular Game Reserve have their own airstrip. These short flights are an experience in itself and very scenic as the aircraft will stay at a low altitude offering you picture perfect views over variety of game reserves in the area.

Getting to Regular Game Reserve by road

There is an excellent tar road from Johannesburg (about a five-hour drive) that leads you to Regular Game Lodge. The last fourteen kilometres inside the Regular Game Reserve concerns an untarred road (can be driven in regular 2 x 4 vehicle).

Regular Game Reserve acess

Regular has one access gate. Access is via Orpen Gate road.Guest will be charged an entrance fee of ZAR 121.00 per adult per day; children pay ZAR 61.00 per day. There is also an additional charge per vehicle (car/ minibus ZAR 50.00) and a community levy applies of ZAR 75.00 per person. These rates are subject to change.Fees are payable at the gate. Game Reserve Gate Opens at 06h00 and closes at 18h00. Should your arrival be later than 16h00 or should you require assistance, please contact your accommodation.
