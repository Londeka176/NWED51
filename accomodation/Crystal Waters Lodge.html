📍Location

Perched on a secluded ridge within a private game reserve in the Kwazulu natal, Crystal Waters Lodge overlooks dramatic sandstone cliffs and lush acacia woodlands.
The lodge sits at the edge of a plateau, offering sweeping views of the valley below and the distant mountain ranges.
