🌙 Moonlight Vale Lodge


🛏️ Accommodation

Two luxurious bedrooms with panoramic views of the Drakensberg peaks. Beautifully appointed living room with natural stone, timber accents, and floor-to-ceiling windows.
Decorative infinity-style pool reflecting the mountain skies. Outdoor dining deck for starlit meals, complemented by an elegant indoor dining area.

🌟 Signature Features

Ranger-driven safari car for daily guided excursions. Infinity decorative pool blending seamlessly with the horizon.
Exclusive outdoor dining with uninterrupted views of wildlife and mountains. Interiors showcasing handcrafted African art and luxury décor.

📍 Location

Nestled in the Drakensberg Mountains, KwaZulu-Natal. Surrounded by dramatic cliffs, lush valleys, and diverse ecosystems. Secluded yet accessible, offering privacy while being close to heritage trails and natural wonders.

🦁 Exclusive Experiences

Daily game drives with expert rangers, offering sightings of antelope, birdlife, and rare mountain fauna. Private outdoor dining under the stars, with menus inspired by local cuisine.
Guided mountain walks to hidden waterfalls and caves. Wellness indulgence by the pool or in the living room with curated wine and cheese tastings.

✨ What Makes Moonlight Vale Lodge Unique

Ultra-exclusive guest focus: only two bedrooms, ensuring privacy and personalized service. Immersive wilderness luxury: blending rugged Drakensberg beauty with refined lodge design.
Signature ranger-led drives: storytelling about the land, heritage, and wildlife. Architectural harmony: lodge design integrates natural materials and open spaces, feeling part of the mountain itself.
