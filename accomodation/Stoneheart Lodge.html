Stoneheart lodge


📍Location

Stoneheart Lodge is perched on a private ridge within the Drakensberg Game Reserve, surrounded by indigenous forest and sweeping views of the Amphitheatre escarpment. The lodge is accessible via a scenic mountain route, offering complete privacy and breathtaking panoramas of the valley below.

🏡 Accommodation

Capacity: Up to 12 guests across 6 luxury suites

Suite Features:

  • King-size beds with Egyptian cotton linens
  • Private decks overlooking the mountains
  • En-suite bathrooms with rain showers and stone finishes
  • Floor-to-ceiling glass walls for uninterrupted views
  • Eco-friendly heating and cooling systems

✨ Signature Features

  • Infinity Pool: Heated outdoor pool with panoramic views of the Drakensberg peaks
  • Living Room: Open-plan lounge with a double-volume ceiling, fireplace, and glass walls framing the valley
  • Dining Area: Elegant indoor-outdoor dining space serving locally sourced cuisine and curated wine pairings
  • Wildlife Viewing Deck: Elevated platform for sunrise and sunset game watching
  • Private Chef & Butler Service: Personalized dining and concierge experience
  • No Spa: Guests rejuvenate naturally through mountain air, tranquility, and outdoor exploration

🌄 View & Ambience

View: Sweeping vistas of the Drakensberg range, valleys, and forest canopy
Ambience: A blend of rustic elegance and modern luxury — lantern-lit evenings, crackling fires, and the distant call of wildlife create a serene, timeless atmosphere
