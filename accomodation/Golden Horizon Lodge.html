🏞 The View

Golden Horizon Lodge is perched high in the Drakensberg Mountains of KwaZulu-Natal, offering uninterrupted vistas of rugged peaks, lush valleys, and star-filled skies.
Guests enjoy sunrise over the mountains and sunset across the rolling landscapes, with the infinity pool and dining area positioned to maximize these breathtaking panoramas.
