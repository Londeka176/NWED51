Regular Game Reserve Lodges

Step into a world where the roar of lions greets the dawn and the stars shine brighter than anywhere else.
At Regular Game Reserve, adventure begins outside your door, and comfort awaits when you return.
Discover the untamed, rest in luxury.

Five lodges, Five journeys of the soul. Each one is a doorway into a different state of mind tranquility, adventure, harmony, heritage, serenity, and renewal.
Here, nature is not just scenery, it is a teacher. The rustle of the grasslands, the call of distant wildlife, and the endless horizon remind you to slow down, breathe deeply, and reconnect with what matters.
Whether you seek the thrill of safari or the quiet of starlit nights, our reserve offers more than a stay—it offers transformation.
Step into the wild, and let the wild step into you.

Facing east, Golden Horizon is designed to capture the first rays of dawn. The atmosphere is radiant, filled with golden light and optimism.
Guests wake to sunlit terraces and breakfast served with mountain honey.

Stoneheart Lodge

Strenght carved into comfort

Built into the side of a mountain, Stoneheart blends raw rock with refined luxury. The atmosphere is grounding,
with fires glowing against granite walls and echoes of ancient strength. Guests dine in halls carved from stone, with flickering torches lighting the way. Beds are granite bases softened with leather headboards, topped with fur throws, offering primal comfort with indulgent softness.
from ZAR 10,000 PER NIGHT PER PERSON

WHISPERS PINES LODGES

Hidden in a grove of towering pines, this lodge feels secluded and magical.
